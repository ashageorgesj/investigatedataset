Research Question 2 What kinds of properties are associated with movies that have high revenues?¶

Let us investigate using the popular_df dataframe. Does popularity and runtime have an effect on revenue? I plan to use revenue_adj as it will give all the years an equal standing.

I had assumed that popularity would have a positive correlationship with revenue. But it does not seem to be the case. Even for high popularities, the revenue is in the mid-range.

Conclusions¶

Drama movies seem to have a slight edge over other genres. Revenue does not seem to have a correlation with popularity while it seems to have a normal shaped distribution with respect to runtime.
